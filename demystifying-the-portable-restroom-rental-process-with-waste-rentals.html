Next up is self-contained bathrooms that require no plumbing connections and don't need to be connected to an external water source. This makes them ideal for outdoor events in remote locations where plumbing isn't available or isn't accessible. These units typically contain additional safety features such as nonslip surfaces and grab bars along the walls so people can easily enter and exit without assistance from another person!
